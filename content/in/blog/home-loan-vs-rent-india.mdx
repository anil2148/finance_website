## Start with affordability, not emotion
If EMI + maintenance + insurance exceeds 35% to 40% of take-home pay, the purchase can stress your monthly cash flow.

## India-specific cost stack
For purchase decisions include:
- Down payment (often 10% to 20%)
- Registration and stamp duty
- Interior and furnishing setup
- Society maintenance and periodic repairs

## Opportunity cost check
Capital parked in down payment could otherwise compound in diversified assets. Model both paths before finalizing.

## Decision triggers
Buy when job stability is strong, emergency corpus is ready, and expected holding period is long.
Rent when flexibility and liquidity are higher priorities.
