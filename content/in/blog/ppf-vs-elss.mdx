## Core difference
PPF offers sovereign-backed stability and long lock-in. ELSS offers market-linked growth potential with a 3-year lock-in.

## 80C planning tip
Use 80C as a portfolio decision, not only a tax deduction checkbox. A blended approach (EPF + PPF + ELSS) can reduce concentration risk.

## When PPF is stronger
- Need lower volatility.
- Want fixed-income diversification.
- Planning long horizon corpus with disciplined yearly contribution.

## When ELSS is stronger
- You have 7+ year horizon.
- You can tolerate equity drawdowns.
- You already have significant fixed-income exposure through EPF/PPF.
