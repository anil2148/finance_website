## SIP projection formula
Future Value = SIP × [((1+r)^n - 1) / r] × (1+r)

## Goal framing in India
Use lakh/crore targets tied to timeline:
- Child education corpus
- Home down payment fund
- Financial independence corpus

## Example
₹15,000 monthly SIP for 15 years at 11% annualized can project around ₹75 lakh (directional estimate).
