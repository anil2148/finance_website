## EMI formula
EMI = P × r × (1+r)^n / ((1+r)^n - 1)

Where:
- P = principal
- r = monthly interest rate
- n = number of monthly installments

## Practical India use case
For a ₹50 lakh home loan at 8.5% for 20 years, monthly EMI is approximately ₹43,391.

## Stress test before borrowing
Run scenarios for:
- +1% interest-rate movement
- 3 months emergency without bonus income
- extra ₹5,000 to ₹10,000 prepayment capacity
