If your goal is less than 3 years away, many households in India prefer the stability of an FD.

For longer horizons, SIPs in diversified equity funds may deliver higher inflation-adjusted growth, but with market volatility.

## Quick decision rule

- **Capital stability priority:** FD
- **Long-term wealth growth priority:** SIP
- **Hybrid:** Keep near-term needs in FD, invest surplus through SIP for long-term goals.

## Tax and liquidity context in India

FD interest is taxed at your slab rate. SIP taxation depends on asset type and holding period.

Use post-tax return assumptions before deciding.
