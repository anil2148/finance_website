## Utilization has two layers
Lenders and scoring models may look at:
- total utilization across all revolving lines, and
- utilization on individual cards.

A single maxed card can hurt even when your overall ratio looks reasonable.

## Practical payment timing approach
- Make a payment before statement close if spending is high that month.
- Keep small recurring charges active on unused cards to avoid inactivity closures.
- Avoid large balance spikes right before mortgage or auto-loan applications.

## Example
If card A has a $2,000 limit and a $1,600 statement balance, that card reports 80% utilization even if total utilization elsewhere is lower. A pre-close payment can reduce reported utilization quickly.
