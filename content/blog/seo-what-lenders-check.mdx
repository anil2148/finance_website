## Clarify the borrowing objective first
Borrowers usually optimize one primary goal:
- lower monthly payment,
- lower total interest,
- or faster approval certainty.
Trying to maximize all three usually backfires.

## Lender checklist and timeline
Typical approval flow:
1. Prequalification (soft pull)
2. Documentation upload (income, assets, obligations)
3. Underwriting and conditional approval
4. Final terms and funding

Delays usually happen because of inconsistent documents, DTI threshold issues, or recent new credit.

## Cost comparison framework
Evaluate loans with:
- APR (not rate alone),
- origination and late-fee structure,
- prepayment flexibility,
- payment-to-income stress test (can you afford this if income drops 15%?).
