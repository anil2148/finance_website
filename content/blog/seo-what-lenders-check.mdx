## The five approval pillars
Most lenders evaluate the same fundamentals:
1. Credit profile and recent behavior
2. Stable, verifiable income
3. Debt-to-income ratio
4. Liquid and reserve assets
5. Complete and consistent documentation

## What borrowers underestimate
- Small inconsistencies between application and statements.
- Recent large account movements without explanation.
- Applying for new credit during underwriting.

## Readiness checklist
- Pull your credit and correct obvious errors.
- Gather income and asset documents in one folder.
- Calculate DTI before applying.
- Avoid major credit or cash-flow changes until close/funding.
