## Start with billing and rate-plan checks
Before buying equipment, verify your utility rate structure, fees, and billing anomalies. Rate-plan mistakes can cost more than small usage changes.

## High-payback actions
1. Fix thermostat scheduling and HVAC filters.
2. Seal air leaks around doors/windows.
3. Shift heavy usage to lower-cost time windows (if TOU plan).
4. Audit water heating settings and insulation.

## Low-payback traps
- Replacing still-functional appliances too early.
- Buying gadgets with unclear savings impact.
- Running multiple overlapping subscriptions/add-ons on utility accounts.
