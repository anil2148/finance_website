## Start with payback-ranked actions
The best utility savings plan prioritizes moves by speed of payoff, not effort alone.

## High-impact actions by category
- **Immediate (0–30 days):** thermostat schedule, leak fixes, LED swaps, usage alerts.
- **Medium-term (1–6 months):** insulation tweaks, HVAC tune-up, appliance cycle optimization.
- **Longer-term:** equipment replacement when lifecycle economics are clear.

## Example household impact
If a home reduces electricity and gas costs by $65/month, annual savings are ~$780. Reinvesting part of that toward emergency savings compounds the benefit.

## Tradeoffs to watch
Some upgrades have long payback periods. Prioritize the biggest recurring reduction per dollar spent.
