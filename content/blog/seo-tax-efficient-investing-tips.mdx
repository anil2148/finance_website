## Start with asset location before advanced tactics
A common win: place tax-inefficient assets (like many bond funds) in tax-advantaged accounts first, and keep tax-efficient equity index funds in taxable accounts.

## Tax-loss harvesting rules that prevent mistakes
Harvesting can help, but only with discipline:
- Realize losses intentionally.
- Avoid wash-sale violations.
- Reinvest into a similar (not substantially identical) allocation.

Done poorly, harvesting adds complexity without meaningful benefit.

## Withdrawal sequencing matters in retirement
The order you tap accounts can materially affect tax bills:
- Use taxable assets strategically.
- Coordinate traditional-account withdrawals with bracket management.
- Preserve Roth flexibility for later years when possible.
