## Use the statement cycle to your advantage
Interest usually starts when statement balances are not paid by the due date. Your monthly workflow should be built around this sequence:
- statement closes,
- balance posts,
- autopay clears before due date.

## A practical setup that works
- Autopay full statement balance.
- Keep a dedicated "card payment buffer" account.
- Use weekly spend check-ins for variable categories.

This reduces the odds of late fees and accidental carrying balances.

## If you already carry debt
Prioritize high-APR balances first and stop new revolving balances. Consider a 0% APR balance transfer only if you have a defined payoff timeline before promo expiry.
