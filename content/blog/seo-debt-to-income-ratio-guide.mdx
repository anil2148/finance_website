## DTI basics in one minute
DTI = monthly debt obligations ÷ gross monthly income.

Many lenders evaluate:
- **Front-end DTI** (housing costs only)
- **Back-end DTI** (housing + other recurring debts)

## Why DTI can block approvals
A high DTI signals repayment risk even with strong credit scores. Lenders look for room in your budget to absorb disruptions.

## Practical ways to improve DTI
- Pay down revolving debt with highest payment impact first.
- Increase reportable qualifying income where possible.
- Refinance or restructure existing obligations before applying.
- Avoid opening new debt accounts 3–6 months pre-application.
