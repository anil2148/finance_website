## The core question
Do you want the tax benefit now (Traditional) or later (Roth)?

- **Traditional IRA:** potential tax deduction today, taxable withdrawals later.
- **Roth IRA:** no deduction today, qualified withdrawals tax-free later.

## Scenario-based examples
- Early-career worker in a lower tax bracket: Roth is often compelling.
- Peak-earning professional expecting lower retirement income: Traditional may make sense.
- Uncertain future taxes: split contributions for diversification.

## Rules that matter before you choose
- Income limits for direct Roth contributions.
- Deduction rules for Traditional IRA when covered by a workplace plan.
- Early-withdrawal penalty and exception rules.

## Common mistakes
- Choosing solely from social posts without checking eligibility.
- Ignoring state tax effects.
- Forgetting to review choice after major income changes.
