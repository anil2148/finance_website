## Start with transfer timing, not transfer size
Most automation fails because transfers happen before bills clear. Align transfers with your payroll pattern first, then increase the amount.

## A setup that survives uneven months
For a biweekly paycheck:
- Payday +1 day: transfer 6% to emergency savings
- Mid-cycle: transfer fixed amount to sinking funds
- Month-end: sweep leftover cash above a floor balance

Example: With $2,200 per paycheck, start with $130 recurring and a $75 month-end sweep.

## Guardrails that prevent overdraft stress
- Keep a checking buffer (for example, $400–$700).
- Pause transfer tiers automatically if balance drops below your floor.
- Review every quarter after rent, insurance, or debt-payment changes.
