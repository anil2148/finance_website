## Build a monthly system around real constraints
Good budgeting and saving plans survive irregular bills, not perfect months. Start with fixed essentials, then automate savings before discretionary spending.

## Practical category planning
Use a baseline split and adjust by local cost pressure:
- Housing + utilities
- Transportation
- Food
- Debt minimums
- Emergency and sinking funds
- Flexible spending

## Where savings plans fail
- Targets are detached from take-home pay volatility.
- Transfers happen too early and trigger overdraft risk.
- No separate buckets for annual/irregular expenses.
