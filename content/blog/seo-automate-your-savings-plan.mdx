## Automation works only when timing matches reality
A transfer plan can fail even with good intentions if it ignores due dates and variable bills. Build around your paycheck calendar first.

## Transfer ladder model
- Day after paycheck: move a small fixed amount to emergency fund.
- Mid-cycle: transfer to sinking-fund buckets.
- End-of-month sweep: move remaining surplus.

This staged system reduces overdraft risk versus one large transfer.

## Safeguards that prevent burnout
- Keep one-bill-cycle cash buffer in checking.
- Start with conservative amounts and increase quarterly.
- Pause only one transfer when needed; do not cancel the entire system.
