In India, buy-vs-rent is often decided emotionally. Use a numbers baseline first:

1. Compare annual rent with annual ownership cost (EMI interest + maintenance + taxes).
2. Include down payment opportunity cost.
3. Stress-test EMI against job/income uncertainty.

## Rule of thumb

If EMI would materially reduce your monthly savings rate, renting can be the financially safer short-term choice.
