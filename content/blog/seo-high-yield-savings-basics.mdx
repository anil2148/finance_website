## APY matters, but access rules matter too
Two accounts with similar APY can feel very different in real life depending on transfer speed, withdrawal limits, and support quality.

## Comparison checklist
- APY and whether it is promotional or ongoing
- Transfer timing to external banks
- Minimum balance requirements
- Mobile app reliability and support channels
- Excess withdrawal policy and account limits

## Example evaluation
Account A: 4.60% APY, slow external transfers.
Account B: 4.45% APY, same-day internal transfers and better support.
For emergency funds, Account B can be the better practical choice.

## Common mistakes
- Chasing tiny APY differences while ignoring friction.
- Splitting funds across too many accounts.
- Keeping no checking buffer and forcing frequent transfers.
