## APY matters, but access rules matter too
The best HYSA for you is not always the highest advertised APY. Also check:
- transfer speed to checking,
- monthly limits or transaction rules,
- minimum balance requirements,
- mobile experience and account alerts.

## A practical bucket strategy
Use multiple savings buckets:
- emergency fund,
- near-term bills (insurance, taxes, car maintenance),
- specific goals (travel, move, education).

This prevents borrowing from your emergency reserve for predictable expenses.

## Common mistakes
- Chasing every short-term APY move.
- Keeping all cash in one account with no purpose labels.
- Ignoring FDIC/NCUA coverage limits across institutions.
