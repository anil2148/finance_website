## Credit-card strategy should start with behavior, not rewards hype
The right card depends on payment habits and utilization control. Rewards only matter if you avoid interest.

## Decision framework for cardholders
- **New/rebuilding credit:** prioritize approval odds, no annual fee, and simple terms.
- **Transactor (pays in full):** maximize category rewards and protections.
- **Revolver (carries balance):** focus on APR, balance transfer math, and payoff speed.

## Score and cost tradeoffs
Key levers:
1. Statement balance vs. credit limit (utilization).
2. Statement closing date and autopay timing.
3. Promo APR expiration and deferred-interest traps.
