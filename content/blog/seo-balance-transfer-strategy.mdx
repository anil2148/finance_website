## Balance transfer math before application
A transfer only helps if you can pay down principal during the promo window. Start with break-even math, not marketing copy.

## Break-even formula
Savings estimate:
- Current card interest avoided over promo period
minus
- Transfer fee (usually 3%–5%)
minus
- Any annual fee.

Example: Transfer $8,000 with a 3% fee ($240). If the old APR is 24%, 12 months of avoided interest can exceed $1,500, making the transfer valuable **if** payments are aggressive.

## Repayment target to stay on track
For a 15-month promo on $8,000, target at least $535/month. Add buffer for any new purchases.

## Risk controls
- Disable new spending on the transfer card.
- Confirm post-promo APR before submitting.
- Set autopay above minimum from day one.
