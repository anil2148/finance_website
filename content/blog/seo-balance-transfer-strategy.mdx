## The only question that matters
Will the transfer fee plus any remaining interest be lower than staying with your current APR?

## Quick break-even approach
1. Calculate transfer fee (for example, 3% of transferred amount).
2. Estimate monthly payoff amount you can sustain.
3. Confirm balance reaches zero before promo APR ends.

If the plan does not clear the balance in time, the transfer may underperform expectations.

## Operational risks to watch
- Late payment can void promo terms.
- New purchases on transfer card can complicate payoff.
- Multiple transfers can increase complexity and error risk.

## Who should skip balance transfers
- Households with unstable monthly cash flow.
- Borrowers without a realistic repayment schedule.
- Anyone likely to keep charging while trying to pay down old balances.
