## Choose your first card by risk management
Your first card should optimize approval odds, low downside, and clean credit-building behavior—not rewards hype.

## Decision framework
Score each option on:
- No annual fee
- Starter approval profile
- Clear autopay tools
- Simple rewards you will actually redeem
- Reasonable late fee policy

## Scenario examples
- **Student with thin file:** secured or student card with no annual fee.
- **New full-time worker:** starter unsecured card with predictable approval criteria.
- **Immigrant/new-to-country profile:** issuer with alternative data support and strong app controls.

## First 90-day operating rules
1. Keep utilization below 10% reported.
2. Turn on full-statement autopay.
3. Make one small recurring charge and one manual mid-cycle payment.
