## The right emergency fund size is risk-based
“Three to six months” is a starting range, not a rule. Pick your target based on income volatility, dependents, and replacement-time risk.

## A practical target formula
Monthly essentials × risk multiplier:
- Stable dual income: 3–4 months
- Single income or variable commissions: 5–6 months
- Self-employed/high volatility: 6+ months

If essentials are $3,400 and you choose 5 months, target = $17,000.

## Build in stages
1. Stage 1: $1,000 quick buffer.
2. Stage 2: 1 month of essentials.
3. Stage 3: full target.

## Where to hold it
Prioritize liquidity and safety over return chasing. High-yield savings is usually the core option.
