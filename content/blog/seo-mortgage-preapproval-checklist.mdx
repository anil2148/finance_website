## Preapproval speed comes from document quality
Most delays happen because income, assets, or debt records are incomplete or inconsistent across documents.

## Document packet to prepare first
- 2 recent pay stubs
- 2 years of W-2/1099 forms
- Recent bank and investment statements
- ID and proof of address
- Debt obligations with minimum payments

## Timeline you should expect
- Days 1–2: initial application and credit pull
- Days 2–5: document review and clarifications
- Days 5–10: conditional preapproval decision

## Common delay triggers
- Large unexplained deposits
- New credit inquiries during review
- Missing pages on statements
