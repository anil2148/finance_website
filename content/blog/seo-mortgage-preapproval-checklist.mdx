## Documents lenders typically request
- Last 2 years of W-2s/1099s and tax returns
- Recent pay stubs and bank statements
- ID and address history
- Debt statements (auto, student, credit cards)
- Gift letter documentation if applicable

Self-employed borrowers usually need additional business documentation.

## Typical timeline
- Day 1–3: initial application and soft/initial credit review
- Day 4–10: document collection and underwriting review
- Day 11+: conditions, clarifications, and preapproval issuance

Timelines vary by lender workload and borrower responsiveness.

## Common blockers
- Large unexplained deposits
- Recent new credit inquiries/accounts
- Inconsistent income documentation
- Debt-to-income ratio above lender comfort range
